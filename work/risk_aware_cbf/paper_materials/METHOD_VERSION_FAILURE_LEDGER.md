# Method Version Failure Ledger

This ledger records what each method version actually tested, what it did not
test, and whether its failure is local to an implementation, configuration,
mechanism, mechanism family, or research direction. A negative result here
freezes the failed version, not the whole direction unless the stop rule in
`REDESIGN_GO_NO_GO_RULES.md` is met.
