## Recommendation

Primary: R1 Verification-Aware Supervisory Mode Selection, pending restoration of
the exact V4-C and baseline-wrapper interfaces required for Stage 0.

Backup: R4 Persistent-Warning Triggered Recovery Coordination.

R2 and R3 remain open, but they carry higher action-semantics and planner
boundary risks. They should not be implemented before R1 clarifies whether
mode selection among already validated components is enough.
